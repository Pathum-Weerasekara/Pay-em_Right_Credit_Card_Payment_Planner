How to use Plaid Link

  1. Make sure you have populated your PLAID_CLIENT_ID, PLAID_SECRET, and PLAID_ENV in your local .env file.
  2. Click the Link a New Bank or Credit Card button on the left.
  3. If in Sandbox environment, select any bank (e.g. Chase) and log in with Plaid's test username: user_good and password: pass_good.
  4. If in Development environment, select your real bank and log in using your real bank credentials. Plaid's UI will securely handle any Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) prompts.
  5. Select which accounts (checking, savings, and credit cards) you want to sync.
  6. After linking successfully, go back to the Dashboard and click the Sync via Plaid button to fetch your real-time balances and monthly transactions!

🔒 Cybersecurity & Privacy

This application implements secure token-exchange architectures recommended by financial cybersecurity standards:

  • Tokenized Logins: Your bank username and password are never sent to or stored by this app. They are securely handled by Plaid Link.
  • Access Token Exchange: We only store a localized, encrypted Plaid access_token which can be easily revoked from your bank dashboard at any time.
  • Strict Offline Design: All data is stored locally in your SQLite database (payment_planner.db) and never uploaded to any cloud server.
