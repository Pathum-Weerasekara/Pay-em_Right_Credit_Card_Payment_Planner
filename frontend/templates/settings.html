How to get YNAB API Token

  1. Log in to your YNAB account on a web browser.
  2. Go to your Account Settings (click your profile in bottom-left corner and choose Settings).
  3. Scroll down to the Developer Settings section and click Manage Developer Settings (or go to app.ynab.com/settings/developer).
  4. Under Personal Access Tokens, click New Token.
  5. Enter your password and a description (e.g. Local CC Planner).
  6. Copy the generated token immediately (it will only be shown once).
  7. Paste the token into the YNAB Personal Access Token field on this page and click Save Settings.
